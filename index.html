Multi-chain offering

LuaStarter supports both Ethereum and TomoChain pools, providing flexible options for investors to participate in both chains simultaneously.

Tier Guaranteed System

TGS model enables investors to be guaranteed in the IDO and utilize all of their tokens. The flexibility of the model also provides opportunities for all investors to buy tokens fairly and efficiently.

Ecosystem Integration

LuaStarter's participants are supported with the whole product suite of LuaSwap running on both Etherum and TomoChain, including bridging, swapping, farming, on-chain limit order, portfolio management.
